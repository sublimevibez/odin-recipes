Cowboy Chicken

Yummy delicious cowboy chicken

The recipe below is pretty straight foward. Feel free to add other ingredients and or substitutes for example bacon.

ingrediants

    boneless skinless chicken
    buffalo wild wing garlic parmesian sauce bottle
    milk or heavy whip cream
    whole cube block of cream cheese
    1 cup of parmesian cheese
    youre choice of pasta noodles

Directions

add youre ingrediants except the pasta noodles. when you add the buffalo sauce refill the bottle with the milk or heavy whip cream. Cook low for 8 hours or on high for 4 hours Shred the chicken half way through if on high shred when 2 hrs to go. If on low shread when 4 hrs to go. when about 30 minutes left add youre pasta noodles and top with cheese.
